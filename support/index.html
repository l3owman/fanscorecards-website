Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create an account using your email address or Sign in with Apple. Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen to get started.

How do I score a fight?

Navigate to an upcoming or live event, select a fight, and use the scoring interface to record your round-by-round scores. Your scorecards are automatically saved to your account.

Can I edit my scorecards after submitting?

Yes, you can edit your scores at any time during the fight. Once a fight is marked as completed, your final scorecard is locked in.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email address above. We will process your request promptly.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Sign in with the same account you used to purchase your subscription. Your subscription status will automatically sync. If you experience issues, use the "Restore Purchases" option in Settings.

Report a Bug

Found something not working correctly? When reporting a bug, please include:

1. A description of what happened

2. What you expected to happen

3. Your device model and iOS version

4. Screenshots if applicable

Send bug reports to luke@fanscorecards.com with "Bug Report" in the subject line.
